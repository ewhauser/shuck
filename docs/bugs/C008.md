## Delta Analysis

### Trap-body span anchoring `[location-only]`

**Bucket:** ShellCheck-only / Shuck-only / Location-only
**Impact:** 12 scripts in the sample, with 18 ShellCheck spans and 20 Shuck spans across the same trap sites

Shuck reports the full quoted trap argument span, while ShellCheck points at the handler substring itself. The rule is detecting the right commands, but the diagnostic anchor is wider than ShellCheck's and makes the locations look different even when the underlying warning is the same.

**Samples:**
- `community-scripts__ProxmoxVE__misc__tools.func` - two trap handlers at lines 1104 and 7847.
- `docker-library__official-images__test__tests__ghost-basics__run.sh` - one `trap` reports three ShellCheck spans from a single quoted handler.
- `dokku__dokku__plugins__openresty-vhosts__command-functions` - three separate handlers show the same anchor drift.
- `gentoo__gentoo__sys-apps__mlocate__files__mlocate.cron-r3` - ShellCheck points at the quoted body; Shuck points at the whole argument.

### Directive suppression miss `[shuck-fix]`

**Bucket:** Shuck-only
**Impact:** 1 script in the sample

`# shellcheck disable=SC2064` suppresses the warning in ShellCheck, but Shuck still emits `C008` for the same trap site. That makes this look like a suppression-path bug rather than a rule-definition mismatch.

**Samples:**
- `gentoo__gentoo__app-antivirus__fangfrisch__files__fangfrisch-has-news.sh:73` - `trap "rm $t" EXIT` remains reported even though the preceding ShellCheck disable comment should suppress SC2064.

### Parse-abort noise `[environment]`

**Bucket:** Environment
**Impact:** 11 scripts in the sample

These failures are parser compatibility issues on non-shell or malformed fixtures in the corpus sample, not evidence that the C008 matcher itself is wrong. They should stay out of the rule-fix queue unless we decide to broaden parser coverage.

**Samples:**
- `GameServerManagers__LinuxGSM__lgsm__modules__alert_ifttt.sh` - unterminated command substitution.
- `HariSekhon__DevOps-Bash-tools__git__git_diff_commit.sh` - unterminated double quote.
- `SlackBuildsOrg__slackbuilds__games__njam__patches__drop_gda.patch` - patch content rather than a shell script.
